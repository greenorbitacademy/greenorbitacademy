About Green Orbit Academy

Green Orbit Academy empowers professionals and organisations to achieve measurable impact through practical learning, mentorship, and leadership development in sustainability, space, and digital transformation.

  1. Home
  2. About

Who we are

Green Orbit Academy is part of the Impact Orbit Creative Group, dedicated to building professional capability across global sustainability, space, and digital sectors. We focus on practical learning pathways that equip individuals and teams to adopt structured impact frameworks and make informed, ethical, and strategic decisions.

Our expertise

Professional experience

Our team brings decades of experience in sustainable marketing, space-sector strategy, digital transformation, and capability development. We combine technical expertise with effective pedagogy to deliver learning experiences that translate complex concepts into actionable insights.

Sector knowledge

With deep understanding of sustainability, impact measurement, and space operations, we help participants integrate responsible innovation into their work. Our approach bridges theory and practice, providing professionals with the tools to implement strategic change and achieve measurable outcomes.

Our approach

Green Orbit Academy focuses on immersive, practical, and data-driven learning. We emphasize ethical communication, inclusive pedagogy, and mentorship, ensuring that participants can confidently apply knowledge in their organisations. By fostering collaboration, experimentation, and leadership, we equip professionals to navigate complex challenges and drive tangible impact.

Leadership

Ryan Laird

Founder & Director, Green Orbit Academy

Ryan leads the Academy within the Impact Orbit Creative Group, shaping its strategic vision for professional development across space, sustainability, and impact sectors. With over a decade of experience in sustainable marketing and space-sector strategy, he designs learning pathways that translate complex concepts into practical skills.

Ryan is a recognised thought leader and mentor, delivering courses, workshops, and masterclasses that blend sector expertise with actionable insights. He actively engages with the wider ecosystem through speaking engagements, advisory roles, and community outreach, fostering inclusive learning and leadership development.
