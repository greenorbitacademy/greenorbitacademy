## Course Overview

This workshop takes you through the complete process of building a brand that communicates clarity, credibility, and long-term purpose. You’ll develop a structured understanding of how brand identity works across strategic, visual, and narrative layers, enabling you to position your organisation effectively in an increasingly competitive market.

### What you’ll gain
- A refined brand purpose and positioning statement  
- A messaging architecture adaptable across teams and channels  
- A high-level visual identity blueprint with sustainability considerations  
- A practical roadmap for rolling out your new or refreshed brand  
- Tools for measuring brand performance and ongoing governance  

### Who this workshop is for
- Early-stage startups needing clear differentiation  
- Scale-ups preparing for investment, growth, or new product launches  
- Space and sustainability organisations seeking stronger narratives  
- Marketing, comms, and strategy professionals refining their brand capability  

### Outcomes
By the end, you’ll have a complete, actionable brand strategy aligned with your mission—ready to activate across campaigns, digital platforms, and stakeholder communications.
