## Course Overview

**Green Orbit Academy** presents the **Transition to Net Zero Workshop**, designed to help businesses translate climate commitments into measurable actions.  

Participants will learn to:

- Understand global Net Zero targets and frameworks  
- Measure, prioritise, and reduce organisational carbon footprint  
- Implement energy efficiency and sustainable supply chain strategies  
- Communicate climate initiatives to stakeholders effectively  
- Track progress and continuously improve climate performance  

**Who Should Attend:**

- Sustainability officers and ESG managers  
- Business leaders and operations managers  
- Strategy and planning teams aiming to embed Net Zero practices  
- Consultants supporting organisations on climate action  

**Key Takeaways:**

- Actionable carbon reduction plans and frameworks  
- Tools to monitor and report Net Zero progress  
- Strategies for stakeholder engagement and ESG alignment  
- Practical examples from organisations successfully achieving Net Zero
