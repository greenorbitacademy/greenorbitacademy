## Course Overview

**Green Orbit Digital** presents the **Sustainability Action Plan Workshop**, designed to help businesses turn sustainability commitments into actionable strategies.  

Participants will learn to:

- Assess organisational sustainability performance using structured frameworks  
- Set measurable environmental and social goals aligned with business priorities  
- Develop a clear, actionable roadmap for sustainability initiatives  
- Integrate ESG and SDG considerations into operations and decision-making  
- Monitor, measure, and report progress to stakeholders effectively  

**Who Should Attend:**

- Sustainability and ESG managers  
- Business leaders and operations managers  
- Strategy and planning teams  
- Consultants supporting organisations in sustainability implementation  

**Key Takeaways:**

- Fully structured sustainability action plan for immediate implementation  
- Tools for tracking and reporting ESG and sustainability KPIs  
- Strategies for engaging internal and external stakeholders  
- Practical examples from companies successfully implementing sustainability initiatives  

> “Sustainability is a business opportunity as much as it is a responsibility. This workshop empowers organisations to take measurable action, create impact, and embed responsible practices into core operations.” – Ryan Laird, Founder & Director, Green Orbit Digital
