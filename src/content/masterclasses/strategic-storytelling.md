## Course Overview

**Green Orbit Academy** presents the **Strategic Storytelling Masterclass**, designed to help teams and professionals craft narratives that influence, engage, and resonate.  

Participants will learn to:

- Analyse audiences and map stakeholders for tailored messaging  
- Structure story arcs to communicate impact and purpose  
- Simplify complex scientific, technical, or policy topics  
- Use data, visuals, and examples to reinforce stories  
- Apply storytelling to ESG, sustainability, and innovation initiatives  

**Who Should Attend:**

- Communications and marketing professionals  
- Policy or science communicators  
- ESG and sustainability officers  
- B2B leaders aiming to enhance engagement through narrative  

**Key Takeaways:**

- Frameworks for persuasive and structured storytelling  
- Messaging templates and practical exercises  
- Techniques for translating complex concepts clearly  
- Skills to increase engagement, trust, and stakeholder alignment
