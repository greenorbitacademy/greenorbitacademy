## Course Overview

**Green Orbit Academy** presents the **Communications Excellence Masterclass**, designed for leaders, spokespeople, and communication professionals seeking to communicate with clarity, authority, and impact.  

Participants will learn to:

- Craft compelling and structured messages for diverse stakeholders  
- Use strategic storytelling to influence and inspire action  
- Simplify complex technical or policy concepts for clarity  
- Enhance executive presence and confidence in media and public settings  
- Apply communications skills to ESG, sustainability, or innovation initiatives  

**Who Should Attend:**

- Senior leaders and executives  
- Communications and media professionals  
- ESG, sustainability, or innovation leads  
- Spokespeople representing science, technology, or policy sectors  

**Key Takeaways:**

- Messaging and storytelling frameworks for leadership communication  
- Media engagement and interview preparation skills  
- Techniques to simplify and deliver complex ideas with authority  
- Practical exercises to build confidence and presence in public settings
