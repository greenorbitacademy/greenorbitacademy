## Course Overview

This masterclass empowers participants to leverage the unique capabilities of space technology to address sustainability challenges on Earth. Through interactive lectures, case studies, and practical exercises, learners will explore:

- Environmental reporting for space missions  
- Energy-efficient satellite operations and debris mitigation  
- ESG compliance and strategic alignment with climate goals  
- Satellite data analysis for terrestrial sustainability projects  
- Designing actionable sustainability strategies for organisations  

Participants will leave with practical tools, frameworks, and industry insights to integrate space and sustainability into their strategic initiatives.
