## Course Overview

**Green Orbit Academy** presents the **Campaign Planning Masterclass**, designed to help marketing and business teams plan, execute, and optimise high-performance campaigns.  

Participants will learn to:

- Define clear objectives and measurable KPIs for campaigns  
- Conduct audience segmentation and research for maximum engagement  
- Craft compelling messaging aligned with brand strategy  
- Select and optimise channels to reach target audiences effectively  
- Analyse campaign performance and make data-driven adjustments  

**Who Should Attend:**

- Marketing managers and teams  
- B2B campaign planners and business development teams  
- Communications professionals seeking structured campaign frameworks  
- Agencies supporting clients in tech and sustainability sectors  

**Key Takeaways:**

- Practical frameworks for campaign planning and execution  
- Templates for messaging, creative strategy, and channel planning  
- Tools for tracking performance and optimising campaigns  
- Insights into integrating ESG and sustainability messaging into campaigns
