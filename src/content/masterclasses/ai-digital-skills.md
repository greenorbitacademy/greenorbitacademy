## Course Overview

This AI & Digital Skills masterclass guides teams and professionals through practical AI applications and digital transformation strategies:

- Explore AI productivity tools to optimise workflows  
- Learn to automate repetitive tasks using modern tools  
- Analyse and visualise data for actionable insights  
- Integrate human-centred design principles into digital solutions  
- Understand digital ethics, bias, and responsible AI  
- Apply skills to real-world business projects  
- Collaborate effectively on AI and digital transformation initiatives  

By the end of the course, participants will have a toolkit of AI and digital strategies ready for immediate implementation.
