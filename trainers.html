Meet the experts driving our programmes

Ryan Laird

Founder & Director, Green Orbit Academy

Ryan leads the Green Orbit Academy within the Impact Orbit Creative Group, shaping its strategic vision for professional development across the global space, sustainability, and impact sectors. He designs learning pathways that empower organisations, startups, and mission-driven teams to adopt structured impact frameworks and integrate sustainable practices.

With over a decade of experience in sustainable marketing and space-sector strategy, Ryan combines sector expertise with pedagogical design to deliver courses, workshops, and masterclasses that are both rigorous and accessible. He champions data-driven decision making, ethical communication, and responsible innovation.

Beyond professional training, Ryan is a recognised thought leader and mentor, actively engaging with the wider ecosystem through speaking engagements, advisory roles, and community outreach. His programmes include accredited certifications, fellowships, and self-paced modules that cultivate leadership, strategic insight, and digital fluency.
